Biography

Where it All Started:

Roughly 7 years ago, I was working for a cabinet shop and somehow managed to cut off about 3/4" of my ring finger on my right hand. After this day, I realized I didn't really want to build cabinets all day, everyday for the rest of my life. I had already received my Associate Degree, but I knew this wouldn't be very helpful in finding a new profession. So I had some decisions to make.

Decisions, Decisions:

I knew that I had to make a decision about my future, but I had no idea what I wanted to do. I just knew that building cabinets all my life wouldn't allow me to reach my full earning potential. So I looked around for several jobs, and tried to get positions at construction, electrical, and roofing companies. But it seemed no one was interested. Finally, I made the decision to go back to school. This wasn't an easy decision, because I had just gotten married, and I was already 27 yrs old. But I decided it was the best option. So I chose to get my BS degree in Technology Management, with and emphasis in Drafting Technology. While going to school, I was able to a job offer with a software company before I even graduated. I was quickly able to work my way up into a more customer focused role. After some time in the customer faced role, I really couldn't see where I was going to go from there to reach the goals I had set for myself. Then it came to me... I decided development was the direction I wanted to go.

Back to School, Back to School, to Prove to Dad that I'm not a fool:

So there I was, now 33 yrs old, heading back to school to learn development. Luckily, the company I worked for was paying for this amazing opportunity... We'll see what the future has in store...
